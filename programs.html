Hybrid" courses are those in which some traditional face-to-face "seat time" has been replaced by online learning activities. The purpose of a hybrid course is to take advantage of the best features of both face-to-face and online learning. Typically, a hybrid course will meet 50% of the time in the classroom and 50% online.
